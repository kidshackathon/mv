Kid Hack Maldives

First Nationwide Hackathon

Date: TBA

Maldives Kid Hack 2020 is a 48 digital innovation event, where kids from every Island will get the opportunity to come together virtually to ideate, hack, design, and develop solutions for their community and nationwide

They will also have the opportunity to attend workshops, one-to-one mentorships, mock pitches during the event, and have fun at the same time!

The hackathon ends after a pitch presentation to a panel of judges made up of industry veterans, pioneers in digital innovations, and notable partners who will decide on the winner.
